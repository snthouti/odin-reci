Smoothie

Description

This is a very easy to make smoothie recipe which will give you enough energy after a good workout session or in the morning for work

Ingredients

  • Dry Fruits
  • Dates
  • Oats
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Banana
  • Cinnamon Powder
  • Chia Seeds

Steps

  1. Put all the dry fruits, two spoons of oats, Chia seeds and Dates in a grinder and grind them well
  2. Peel a Banana or two with Greek Yogurt and grind them again
  3. Finally put milk and ice inside the grinder and grind one last time
  4. Your drink is ready that easily with a lot of fibre and protein
